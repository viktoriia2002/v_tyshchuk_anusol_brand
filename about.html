HISTORY

The idea of ​​creating Anusol beer first appeared in 1834 by the bridge engineer Frederic Anusol, who, exhausted by daily difficult work, decided to develop a unique recipe for a beer drink that would help people of all segments of the population feel as comfortable as possible. And only in 2020 Frederick’s dream came true, and the beer was released and found its admirers.

PHILOSOPHY

The philosophy of our brand is to combine the core values of the brand into a kaleidoscope of impressions: comfort, freedom, naturalness and peaches … Each bottle of Anusol beer gives a feeling of relief.

OUR GOALS

The goal of our brand is to rid the world of the injustice of this world and bring peace, comfort and relief from pain to every home. Anusol is the highest quality beer that will delight you.

THE BREWERY

We can endlessly talk about our super-technological production based on the natural method of brewing, which was used by our great-grandfathers. The company closely monitors compliance with all standards and norms, and also pays special attention to environmental friendliness of production.

OUR TEAM

Our team of like-minded people are happy to work on developing our brand. We were lucky to gather talented enthusiasts under our roof, ready to do anything for the sake of beer!

NEWS

We are proud to announce that our production director Mikhail Grom has won the international competition Brewer 2020! Congratulations to Mikhail and we wish you continued success!
